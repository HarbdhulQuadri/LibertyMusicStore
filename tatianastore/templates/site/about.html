About Liberty Music Store

Liberty Music Store is a service for selling your music and receive Bitcoin payments for them. Sign up, upload songs, sets prices and then embed Liberty Music Store on your website.

History

Liberty Music Store is my weekend hacking project. It started as a proof-of-concept to show how well Bitcoin adapts to online digital content sales. I wanted to do something in Bitcoin space, which is driven mostly by my curiosity towards the upcoming virtual currency revolution. On my travels I met some interesting artists with mindbogging ideas and here we are.

The product is 100% working, though some more work is needed to make it open for the great masses. Thus I'll keeping the registration private until there are sales at least with 3-4 different artists who have given feedback about the service usability. I am especially little proud all the work that went to make Bitcoin payment process as easy as possible.

If you have any ideas where this project could head to please drop an email.

The source code is 100% open source and available on Github.
