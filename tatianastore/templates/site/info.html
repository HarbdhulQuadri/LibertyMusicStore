Frequently asked questions

How can I sign up?

The sign up is currently open by request only. Please drop me an email and I'll create a user account for you if you are an artist who is enthusiastic about Bitcoin. We are looking for more beta users.

How can I add Liberty Music Store on my site?

After the sign up, you can upload your songs and name their prices. Then you'll get HTML embed code which you can add on your site. It works similar to Google Analytics, Facebook Like box and other embedded web components technology. See the front page for examples. Because back office still needs some polishing, let's have an walkthrough in Skype how to upload songs and set the prices for the content.

How much fee the store takes?

Currently the artist can keep 100% of the purchase price. I am not doing this for money. However if the thing catches fire, I'll set 15% fee to cover the cost of running the service.

How Bitcoin works for buying music?

People can purchase Bitcoins through various means (credit card, cash, ATM.) Bitcoins can be purchased in any country, even in countries where PayPal, iTunes et. al. don't work. Purchased Bitcoins can be then used to buy all kinds of stuff. Bitcoin transactions are very cheap and scale globally - they fit quite well for doing small payments for the online content. After people obtain their first Bitcoins and Bitcoin wallet, the purchase process is much sleeker and safer than with credit cards. With Bitcoin, you are in control of your money, no one can take it away. As long as Bitcoins stay within Bitcoin the economy, no hidden fees incur. If you cash out Bitcoins back to fiat currency (USD, EUR, GBP) you may need to pay some fee, usually few percents, for the exchange process.
