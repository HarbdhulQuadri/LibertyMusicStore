Making Bitcoin purchases

Getting Bitcoins

First you need to get some Bitcoins by buying them. The following two sites are recommended:

  • U.S: CoinBase
  • Other countries: LocalBitcoins

Initially you need to buy Bitcoins for a moderate amount. Thi might be too much for a single song purchase. The usual minimum purchase amount is in range of 20 USD. However, you can later spend Bitcoins on other digital services, or turn them back to cash, as Bitcoins are universal.

Bitcoin exchange rate varies daily. You can check the current market exchange rate on a site like Bitcoinity.

Making the payment

Bitcoin payment is made by sending Bitcoins from your Bitcoin wallet to the unique Bitcoin address given on the purchase. It takes 15 - 60 seconds for the network to confirm your Bitcoin payment. After this you can download your purchases immediately.

Desktop web browser: Copy-paste Bitcoin address and Bitcoin amount into your Bitcoin wallet and send the payment from there.

Mobile application wallet: Scan the presented QR code with your Bitcoin wallet app barcode scanner.

Receiving Bitcoin payments

You don't need anything Bitcoin-related to get started on Liberty Music Store. If you do not have a Bitcoin wallet your Bitcoins are held on Libery Music Store until you get one.

To receive Bitcoins you need a Bitcoin wallet. You can get one by signing up Bitcoin services. Examples:

  • U.S: CoinBase
  • Other countries: LocalBitcoins

Setting up your Bitcoin wallet details

Log in to the your Liberty Store Management. Copy-paste Receiving Bitcoin address from your Bitcoin wallet and save it in your store settings.

Liberty Music Store credits Bitcoin purchases daily. You can check the purchase and Bitcoin credit status in Download transaction section of the store management.
