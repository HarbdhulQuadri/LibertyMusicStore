Upload an album

  • The album must be a ZIP file and must contain songs as MP3
  • It is recommended that filenames are in the order they appear on the album. E.g. 01-first-song.mp3, 02-second-song.mp3
  • Add ID3 tags to MP3s to have a song title
  • The ZIP file can contain a JPEG image which is us used as the album cover art

You can edit all files, information, prices and song order after the upload.

Warning: The upload and processing song files may take up to 10 minutes. Just don't close the page when the files are being processed.
